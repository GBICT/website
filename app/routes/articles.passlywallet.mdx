PasslyWallet turns loyalty cards into digital passes that sit right inside Apple Wallet and Google Wallet. No app to download, no plastic card to lose. Your customers get their card by email, tap once to add it, and it's always in their pocket. You get a loyalty programme that runs itself.

## What is PasslyWallet

Most loyalty programmes fail for the same two reasons: nobody downloads yet another app, and paper stamp cards end up in a drawer. So the points never get collected and the customer never comes back.

PasslyWallet removes the friction. The loyalty pass lives in the wallet app every customer already has on their phone. There's nothing to install and nothing to carry. You set up your programme in minutes, your customers join with one tap, and from that moment every visit, every point and every reward is tracked automatically.

One platform, one dashboard, every customer, in the wallet they open ten times a day.

## What PasslyWallet does

**Digital passes in Apple & Google Wallet.** Your branded loyalty card, delivered to the customer by email and added to their phone with a single tap. Points, stamps and rewards update on the card itself.

**Points, stamps and rewards.** Run it your way: collect-and-redeem points or a classic stamp card. Customers scan, earn and cash in rewards, all handled for them.

**Push campaigns.** Reach your customers straight on their phone. A quiet Tuesday, a birthday, a comeback offer for someone who hasn't visited in a while. Send it and bring them back in.

**Automations that run themselves.** Welcome messages, birthday rewards and win-back offers fire automatically, so you keep customers coming back without touching a thing.

**Revenue & ROI tracking.** See exactly what your loyalty programme brings in: visits, redemptions and revenue in one dashboard. No guessing whether it's worth it.

**Referral & POS connection.** Let happy customers bring in new ones, and connect PasslyWallet to your point of sale so points are earned at the counter, automatically.

## How it works

**1. Set up.** Create your programme and brand your pass. No developers, no hardware. You onboard yourself in minutes.

**2. Invite.** Your customers receive their pass by email and add it to Apple or Google Wallet with one tap. No app, no plastic.

**3. Grow.** From there PasslyWallet runs the loop for you: earning points, sending campaigns, firing automations and showing you exactly what it brings back.

## Built for businesses that live on repeat customers

PasslyWallet is made for hospitality and retail: restaurants, cafés, gyms, shops. Anywhere a returning customer is worth far more than a one-off. If your business runs on people coming back, this is how you make sure they do.
