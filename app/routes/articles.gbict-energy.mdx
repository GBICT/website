A home battery only saves you real money if it charges and discharges at exactly the right moment. Doing that by hand, every hour, on prices that change all day, nobody manages that. And every manufacturer only steers its own hardware: Tesla optimises your Powerwall, SolarEdge optimises SolarEdge. Mix brands, and you're on your own.

GBICT Energy fixes both. It connects your battery, your solar and your dynamic energy contract, and lets AI steer everything automatically on the live hourly power price. It's not a dashboard that shows you the price and leaves you to act. It does the acting for you.

You pick the hardware. Our AI handles the rest.

## What GBICT Energy does

**AI-driven optimisation.** The AI pulls the live EPEX hourly prices, forecasts your solar yield and learns your household's usage patterns, then calculates the smartest charge-and-discharge schedule for your battery, sends the commands itself and re-optimises every hour as reality changes. Charge at €0,04, sell at €0,30.

**Learns your home and looks ahead.** GBICT doesn't just react. It predicts. The AI forecasts what you'll use and generate tomorrow, spots unusual consumption before it costs you, and keeps getting sharper the longer it runs on your data.

**Works with every brand, no lock-in.** Tesla Powerwall, Sessy, Victron, SolarEdge, Enphase. You don't replace a thing. Where other platforms only steer their own hardware, GBICT steers all of it.

**A savings dashboard that proves it.** One number up front: what you saved today, this month, since day one. Plus the payback time on your battery. Seeing is believing.

**Full transparency.** Handing control to AI makes people nervous, so we show every decision: "Your battery is discharging now because the price is high, you're saving €1,23 this hour." Nothing hidden in a graph.

**From saving to earning.** First you save on your bill. Next, we bundle your battery back to the grid (VPP) so you earn on grid flexibility too.

On 1 January 2027, net metering ends. From that point, solar panels alone earn you far less, unless you store your power and sell it at the right moment. A battery stops being a luxury and becomes simple logic. GBICT Energy is what turns that battery into money.
