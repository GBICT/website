**Results, engineered.**

GBICT is a software, SaaS and AI company. We build technology that pays for itself: time recovered, costs eliminated, revenue unlocked and operations that finally run without friction.

Every project starts with one question: what does this actually deliver? Not in features. Not in hours. In euros saved, hours won back and processes that run themselves, often with AI doing the heavy lifting in the background. If we can't make that concrete before we start, we don't build it.

## What we do

We operate across three disciplines. Knowing exactly which is which keeps every project sharp.

**Embedded IT.** We integrate directly into your team and drive change from the inside. No software to purchase, no overhead to manage. Just the right people in the right place, keeping your operation running and improving it while they're there.

**Custom software and AI-driven applications.** We design, build and ship: mobile apps, platforms, internal tools and AI automation tailored to how your business actually works. After launch we stay on board to maintain, extend and improve, so what we deliver keeps performing long after day one.

**SaaS products built for you.** We design and develop complete SaaS products for our clients, from first concept to a live, scalable platform. Whether you want to launch a new digital product or turn an internal process into a subscription-ready service, we handle the architecture, the build and the ongoing operation, so your product grows with your business.

## Proven in practice

The pattern is almost always the same: too many tools, too much manual work, too much time lost every single day. We replace that with one system that fits.

Recent results include a client saving significantly on monthly software licenses by consolidating four separate tools into one custom app, an ordering process cut down to seconds with zero errors and full customer insight, and a company replacing expensive per-seat AI licenses with its own internal AI tool, keeping costs low and data fully in-house.

## Our approach

Most of the work starts the same way: a process that is messy and slow, spread across four different tools, held together by phone calls and sticky notes. We turn that into one clear digital system.

But we never start by building. We start by understanding. We research your organisation in depth and uncover the pain points you run into every day: where processes slow down, where friction builds up, where potential goes untapped. Based on that analysis, we determine how SaaS, custom software or AI can be engineered to fit seamlessly within your company, aligned with how your business truly operates. Honest, well-founded advice comes first, always.

Only then do we show you a clickable demo, because you should see it, not just hear about it. Once it fits, we build a working prototype, develop the real thing and deliver it ready to use. After that we stay on for support and improvements.

It means you're never buying a promise on a slide. You're looking at the result before you commit.
