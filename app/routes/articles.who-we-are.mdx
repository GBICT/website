**We build technology that earns its place.**

GBICT is a Dutch IT company with more than six years of experience, a software, SaaS and AI studio based in Almere. We are a small team of five with one shared conviction: software is only worth building when it gives something real back. Time recovered, costs that disappear, revenue that starts to move, and an operation that finally runs without friction.

Being small is the point, not a limitation. You work directly with the people who decide and who build, with no account layer and no helpdesk queue in between. We bring in a trusted network of developers per project, so we stay lean and scale up the moment the work asks for it.

## What we believe

Most businesses we meet are drowning in tools that almost fit and processes that almost work. Before we write a single line of code, we ask what a piece of software will actually deliver, and we answer it in plain terms. Not in features, not in hours on an invoice, but in money saved and time handed back to your team.

We sell outcomes, never hours. And we stay honest to a fault. Sometimes the right advice is a smaller first step, or to fix what you already have instead of replacing it. We would rather tell you that than sell you something you do not need.

## What we do

**Embedded IT.** We step inside your team and improve the operation from within, with nothing to purchase and no overhead to manage.

**Custom software and AI.** We design, build and ship the thing itself: apps, platforms and internal tools shaped around how your business works, with AI doing the heavy lifting in the background. After launch we stay on to maintain and improve it.

**Our own SaaS.** Alongside client work we build our own products and bring them to market, which keeps our craft sharp and our thinking ahead.

## Where we are heading

GBICT grows every day, project after project, for one simple reason: the work speaks for itself. If you are sitting on a process that costs more time and money than it should, that is exactly what we fix. [Send us a message](/contact) and we will show you what it could look like.
