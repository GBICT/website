## Who we are

GBICT is a software, SaaS and AI studio based in Amsterdam. We build smart software for our clients and we bring our own products to market. Behind GBICT are two founders and a tight network of developers we scale up per project, so we stay fast and flexible without carrying overhead our clients end up paying for.

Our ambition is simple: build software that makes a real difference, and become a name people trust in the market.

## What we believe

We don't sell hours. We sell outcomes: time saved, costs cut, revenue grown, and calm in the day-to-day operation.

Every project answers one question: what does this actually deliver for the client? Not in features, not in hours, but in euros saved, hours won back, and processes that finally run themselves. If we can't make that concrete, we don't build it.

## How we work — three tracks

We do three different things, and being clear about which is which keeps every project sharp.

**Embedded IT.** We join your team and make the difference from the inside. No software to buy, just the right people in the right place, keeping your operation running and improving it while they're there.

**Custom software and apps.** We design, build and ship: mobile apps, platforms, internal tools. Then we stay on to maintain and extend them, so the thing keeps working long after launch.

**Our own products.** Alongside client work we build our own SaaS products and bring them to market, from energy optimisation to loyalty and compliance tooling.

## Our approach

Most of the work starts the same way: a process that is messy and slow, spread across four different tools, held together by phone calls and sticky notes. We turn that into one clear digital system.

The path is always the same. First we understand the pain in your words, not ours. Then we show you a clickable demo, because you should *see* it, not just hear about it. Once it fits, we build a working prototype, develop the real thing, and deliver it ready to use. After that we stay on for support and improvements.

It means you're never buying a promise on a slide. You're looking at the result before you commit.

## The sectors we know

We've delivered in hospitality and food, premium and luxury services, construction, healthcare, logistics, and media. Different worlds, same pattern underneath: too many tools, too much manual work, and time leaking out of the process every day.

A few examples of what that looks like in practice:

- A construction company replaced four separate tools with one app, saving around **€3,200 a month** in licences and roughly **8 hours a week** of admin per project manager.
- A hospitality group cut ordering down to **45 seconds**, no queue, no mistakes, and finally got real insight into what their customers order.
- A media company swapped per-seat AI licences for its own in-house AI tool, keeping costs down and its data inside the building.

We want GBICT to become an established name in software, with our own products live and running for paying customers, and a client base that keeps growing because the work speaks for itself.
