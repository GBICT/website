Seaflow is the water intelligence platform that keeps your business compliant and your water bill under control. It connects to the meters you already have and turns raw consumption into real-time insight, early warnings and audit-ready compliance, so you always know exactly where your water goes, before it costs you money or a fine.

## What is Seaflow

Water is one of the most overlooked cost drivers in business. It flows invisibly through your operation, and by the time a leak, an overage or a compliance breach shows up on an invoice or in an inspection, the damage is already done.

Seaflow makes water visible and keeps you inside the rules automatically. The platform connects to your existing water meters, continuously monitors every litre that moves through your business, and turns that stream of data into something you can act on: live insight, instant alerts, consumption forecasts and compliance reports that generate themselves.

Behind the scenes, Seaflow runs on three layers of AI working together. One spots anomalies the moment they start, one forecasts what you are about to use, and one keeps you inside the rules and turns your data into ready-to-submit reports. Together they turn a raw stream of meter readings into decisions, warnings and compliance you can rely on.

It's not another dashboard you have to watch. It's an autopilot that watches for you.

## What Seaflow does

**Automated compliance.** Water scarcity regulation is tightening fast, and exceeding your limits can mean serious fines. Seaflow tracks your consumption against the rules that apply to you, warns you well before you cross the line, and generates the reports authorities, auditors and partners ask for. Accurate, complete and ready to submit, without anyone burning a day on spreadsheets.

**Leak detection, before it becomes damage.** Seaflow recognises abnormal consumption the moment it starts. A valve dripping at night, a burst pipe in an empty wing, a system silently overusing. You get an alert immediately, not a shock on your next invoice.

**Real-time monitoring.** See your water consumption live, across every location, building or zone, from one central view. Updated continuously, accessible from anywhere.

**Consumption forecasting.** Seaflow learns how your business uses water and predicts what you'll need. Spot upward trends early and act before consumption gets out of hand.

## How it works

**1. Connect.** Seaflow works with the meters you already have, wirelessly, through an integration with your system, or via a simple file. No rip-and-replace, no building full of new hardware. On an older meter, at most a small sensor is added. We start by looking at what you already have, so we know exactly what's needed.

**2. Monitor.** The platform builds a live picture of your consumption and learns your normal patterns, per location, per zone, per moment of the day.

**3. Act.** From there Seaflow works for you around the clock: detecting anomalies, forecasting demand, guarding your regulatory limits and generating the reports you need, automatically.
